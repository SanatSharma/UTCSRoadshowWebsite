WHAT WE DO

We go to local K-12 schools and give a thirty minute to one hour presentation to expose students to what Computer Science is, why Computer Science is important, and how kids can prepare for a career within Computer Science. Our presentation is suitable for students of all ages, and our hope is to get students excited about Computer Science!

WHAT IS CS?

We help introduce and teach the concept of Computer Science to kids with our presentation and with a group activity.

WHY CS?

We help introduce and teach the concept of Computer Science to kids with our presentation and with a group activity.

HOW TO PREPARE?

We also introduce local programs and camps that students can attend, and talk about what classes they can take before their college career.

WHO WE ARE

We create presentation content- anything from web content, videos, and powerpoints. Our goal is to create an effective and exciting presentation!
We are responsible for going to local schools to give our presentation. We are a group of well-trained students that are ready and willing to spread the word about Computer Science!
